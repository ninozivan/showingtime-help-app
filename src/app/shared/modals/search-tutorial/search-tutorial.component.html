How to use Quick Search
How does it work?

Just think in form of creating a sentence. Usually you know what you want to do and the area where you want to perform that action.

For an example:

"Within the My Home by ShowingTime, I want to Create an Appointment."

In the above example, My Home by ShowingTime is your Area of interest, Create is the Action and the Appointmentis the Object on which you want to perform the action.

Keep in mind that all fields are optional, you can skip selecting any field and you will get search results matching your selection.
Area of interest?

In most cases you right away know what is your area of interest. It could be some of our products or maybe something generally related to the ShowingTime company (Contact, Marketing, etc.).

Your Action?

Simply what do you want to do. Maybe to Add, Create, Delete or perform some other action within the selected Area of interest.

Your action
Object?

Object on which you want to perform some action. If you want to "Create an Appointment", then "Appointment" is the object of your action.
On other case, if you want to "Contact a Client" than "Client" is the object.
