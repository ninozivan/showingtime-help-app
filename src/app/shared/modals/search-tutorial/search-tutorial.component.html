How to use Quick Search

How do Quick Search works?

Just think in form of creating a sentence. Usually you know what you want to do and the area where you want to perform that action.

For example:

"Within the My Home by ShowingTime, I want to Create an Appointment."

In the above example, My Home by ShowingTime is your Area of interest, Create is the Action and the Appointment is the Object on which you want to perform the action.

Area of interest?

In most cases, you right away know what is your area of interest. It could be some of our products or maybe something generally related to the ShowingTime company (Contact, Marketing, etc.).

Area of interest

Your Action?

Simply what do you want to do. Maybe to Add, Create, Delete or perform some other action within the selected Area of interest.

Your Action

What is the Object?

An Object on which you want to perform some action. If you want to "Create an Appointment", then "Appointment" is the object of your action.
In other cases, if you want to "Contact a Client" then "Client" is the object.
